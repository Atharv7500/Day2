Amrutvahini College of Engineering (AVCOE), established in 1983, is a premier autonomous engineering institute affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and approved by AICTE. Located in a serene 25-acre campus, AVCOE is accredited by NBA and NAAC with an ‘A’ grade, reflecting its commitment to academic excellence and quality education. The college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, with a curriculum that is regularly updated to match industry trends and technological advancements.
